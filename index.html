Pro Gaming Tips

Learn the roles and characters in your preferred games.

Have an idea about your character's strengths and weaknesses.

Connect socially with other gamers.

Online gaming & teamwork always give you more social connections and benefits.

Take care of your body and mind.

Make sure to have a break while gaming and calm your mind and body.

Safety Tips

Secure your computer.

Make sure your firewall is up to date so no one can break into your computer, send viruses, or gain access to your sensitive information.

Stick to your username.

Never give out your name or personal information when playing with somebody you don't know.

Report harassment and abuse.

Tell an adult if a player does something that makes you uncomfortable. To prevent them from contacting you again, use the block and report functions.
